Walk/Ride Day

Everyone is welcome to check in a commute in honor of Walk/Ride Day!

Walk/Ride Days take place on the last Friday of each month, and provide a shared day for everyone in the community to celebrate, try out, or just think about healthy, environmentally sustainable commuting options.

Don’t work on Fridays? Check in another day! You can check in as early as the Monday prior or as late as the Monday after Walk/Ride Day.
